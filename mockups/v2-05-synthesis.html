A Working Belief

What I'm working toward.

The most interesting AI work over the next decade won't happen at the frontier of model capability — it'll happen at the seam between capable models and real industries that don't yet know how to use them.

"
Good systems disappear. The image gets sorted, the bad shot gets dropped, the color comes out right — and nobody asks how. That gap, between a model and a system that quietly works, is where I spend my time.
— Mahdiyar, on building applied AI
