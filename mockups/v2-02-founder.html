§ How I think

A few things I've come to believe.

Operating principles for doing applied AI research that ships.

01

Ship the model into the seam.

The interesting frontier isn't the model — it's the gap between a capable model and an industry that doesn't know how to use it yet.

02

Generalization is a product feature.

If your model only works on the data you trained on, you don't have a model — you have a memorization machine with a license cost.

03

The best systems disappear.

The shot gets sorted, the bad image gets dropped, the color comes out right — and nobody asks how. That quiet machinery is the work.
